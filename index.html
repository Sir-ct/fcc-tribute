Tesla c 1896

Here's a timeline of Tesla's life

  • 1856 - Born in smiljan, croatia
  • 1861 - Attended primary school in Smiljan where he studied German, arithmetic, and religion.
  • 1862 - Moved to nearby Gospić with his family
  • 1870 - Moved to karlovac and attended high school at the Higher Real Gymnasium
  • 1873 - Returned to smiljan, contracted cholera and was bedridden for months, where his father promised to send him to the best engineering school if he recovered from the illness
  • 1874 - Evaded conscription into the Austro-Hungarian Army in Smiljan by running away southeast of Lika to Tomingaj, near Gračac.
  • 1875 - Enrolled at Austrian Polytechnic in Graz on a Military Frontier scholarship, never missed any exam in his first year and got the highest grades possible
  • 1877 - Lost his scholarship, becomes addicted to gambling and gambles away his allowance and tuition money, recovers some of it through gambling also.
  • 1878 - Left Graz and severed all relations with his family to hide the fact that he dropped out of school.
  • 1879 - Refuses bids by his father to return home, suffers a nervous breakdown, returned to Gospić under police custody for lack of residence permit, lost his father on april of the same year.
  • 1881 - moves to Budapest, Hungary, to work under Tivadar Puskás at a telegraph company, the Budapest Telephone Exchange.
  • 1882 - Got a job through Tivadar Puskás at a new industry named continental Edinson company, installing indoor incandescent lighting citywide in the form of electric power utility
  • 1884 - Emigrated to the united states and began working at the machine works in manhattan's lower east side, troubleshooting installations and improving generators, developed arc-light based lighting system, wasn't paid promised bonus and leaves the edinson company
  • 1885 - Obtained patents for his arc lighting system and his improved DC generator, met with Robert lane and Benjamin vail who financed his company "Tesla Electric light & manufacturing"
  • 1886 - Robert and Benjamin split the company leaving him penniless. met Alfred S. Brown, a Western Union superintendent and New York attorney Charles Fletcher Peck.
  • 1887 - Brown and Peck finance him to start "Tesla Electric company". Developed an electric motor that run on AC current
  • 1889 - Invented the tesla coil
  • 1895 - His lab got burnt destroying most of his works in development.
  • 1943 - He dies at the age of 86

"I hold that space cannot be curved, for the simple reason that it can have no properties. It might as well be said that God has properties. He has not, but only attributes and these are of our own making. Of properties we can only speak when dealing with matter filling the space. To say that in the presence of large bodies space becomes curved is equivalent to stating that something can act upon nothing. I, for one, refuse to subscribe to such a view"
--Tesla
